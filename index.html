Adventure of Pepe

The adventure of Pepe starts here, are you willing to help him out on his journey?

El Pollo Loco is a classic 2D video game. Our hero "Pepe" has to fight against his enemys and kill them. On his way to defeat the Endoss, Pepe has to collect salsa-bottles beeing able to throw at & kill the endboss. His way is full of angry chickens & collectables. When Pepe won the fight against the endboss, he will meet his Senorita and enjoy the evening with her.
