The adventure of Pepe starts here, are you willing to help him out on his journey?

El Pollo Loco is a classic 2D video game. Our hero "Pepe" has to fight against his enemys hand kill them. On his way to defeat the Endoss, Pepe has to collect salsa-bottles beeing able to throw & kill the endboss. His way is full of angry chickens & collectables. Once Pepe collected 10 coins he can exchange them for abit x-tra health. Once Pepe won the fight against the endboss, he will meet his Senorita and enjoy the evening with her.
